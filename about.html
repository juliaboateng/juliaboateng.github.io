Hello! I'm Julia Boateng.

I'm a UI/UX designer who's passionate about visual design and creating meaningful solutions.

I’m currently a self employed content creator looking to break into UX/UI design. I run a Youtube channel which currently has over 78,000 subscribers where i do breakdowns and discussions on issues in pop culture.

Currently, I hold a bachelor’s degree in psychology, and while I didn’t know it at the time, that was truly my first foray into the world of user experience, as psychology is all about understanding the behavior and motivations of a person.

After graduating, I worked in social media for some time and then entered a master’s program for Communication Studies. It was while doing this program that i reconnected with an old friend who was looking to pursue UX/UI design. After talking with her and doing some research about UX/UI, I fell in love with the field. I realized that it combined all of my skills up until this point- communications, psychology, digital design, storytelling (with my YouTube videos!) and more. If anything, i was most intrigued by the fact that UX/UI has the designer constantly asking “why.” How is this design solution truly going to benefit the user, what problem are we looking to solve?

To pursue this new career, I enrolled in Careerfoundry’s UI design bootcamp, while continuing to manage my growing social media presence.
